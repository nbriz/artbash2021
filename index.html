ARCHIVE UNDER CONSTRUCTION: ArtBash is the Department of Contemporary Practices' major end of the year exhibition of student work developed out of their interdisciplinary studio courses in the department. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the ArtBash 2020 and 2021 exhibition took place entirely online at this domain, artbash.party. SAIC is currently working on creating a permanent archive of the 2020 and 2021 virtual galleries.
